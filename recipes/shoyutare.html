Shoyu Tare

What is shoyu tare?

Shoyu tare is a soy sauce based seasoning used to flavour ramen broth. It is also used to marinate eggs if you're using them as a topping.

Ingredients (for four)

  • 75ml soy sauce
  • 35ml water
  • 10ml dry sake
  • 10ml mirin
  • 10g fine sea salt
  • 5g granulated sugar

Steps

  1. Mix the dry sake, mirin, water and soy sauce in a saucepan and bring to a boil.
  2. Add salt and sugar and stir to dissolve.
